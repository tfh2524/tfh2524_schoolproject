Fantasy

Fantasy is one of those genres that more apply to movies or books in the consumers' eyes. But almost every game has some sort of fantasy like element to them but not all of them are fantasy games. For example, games that are fantasy have fictional characters and places along with fictional monsters and stories. Among these games are those that are recognized by their name association with the genre, such as Final Fantasy. These games are indeed Fantasy as they have a fictional story in a fictional setting.
